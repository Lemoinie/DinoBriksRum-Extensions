# DinoBriks Brews Guide

The DinoBriks Rum datapack contains 8 distinct brewing bases. Each base can be mutated by tossing specific catalyst items near the brewing barrel during the fermentation process.

Here is the comprehensive list of all bases, catalysts, and final distilled beverages.

---

## 1. Absinthe
**Base Ingredient:** 16x Fern

| Variant | Catalyst Items (Tossed nearby) | Final Distilled Product |
|---------|--------------------------------|-------------------------|
| **Standard Absinthe** | *None* | Absinthe |
| **Ghastly Cry** | 4x Ghast Tear | Ghastly Cry |
| **Pinewood Absinthe** | 4x Spruce Sapling, 4x Ice | Pinewood (Unaged) |
